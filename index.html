01 - Control System · Engineering Lead

Water Treatment Plant, end-to-end.

Led the full engineering lifecycle of a mini WTP - P&ID, control narratives, panel layouts, wiring diagrams, and core control logic across Arduino, Raspberry Pi, PLC, HMI and SCADA.

Problem
Investor needed a fully integrated control system spanning multiple engineering disciplines and hardware layers.
Action
Produced all engineering drawings from P&ID to wiring diagrams. Built filtration, backwash, drain, and safety sequencing on Arduino; supervised Pi/PLC/HMI/SCADA integration.
Result
WTP with reliable multi-platform communications and a clean separation of control layers across hardware.

02 - AutoCAD · Field Verification

As-built documentation for Geo Dipa Energy.

Field-verified and redrew engineering documents for PT Fusi Gama Indonesia under a Geodipa project managed by Kwarsa Hexagon.

Problem
P&ID references did not match installed field instruments - discrepancies were blocking commissioning sign-off.
Action
Direct field verification of tag numbers, signal types and physical positions. Redrew hook-up drawings and instrument location layouts in AutoCAD from verified field data.
Result
Accurate as-built documentation matching real field conditions - all identified discrepancies resolved.

03 - PLC & Automation · Assistant Instructor

Teaching PLC, at Universitas Gadjah Mada.

Delivered hands-on PLC programming sessions for the Teknik Fisika department at UGM covering Siemens S7-1200, WinCC HMI and Schneider PLC (Aug–Dec 2025).

Scope
Designed custom case studies covering analog scaling, signal processing and practical control logic across two PLC platforms.
Action
Configured S7-1200 ↔ WinCC comms, guided students through ladder-logic builds, supported troubleshooting on both Siemens and Schneider hardware.
Impact
Students developed the confidence and skills to diagnose and resolve control-system issues independently on both platforms.
